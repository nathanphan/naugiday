Let's Cook

1

Prepare the aromatics

Peel the garlic cloves. Using a mortar and pestle, pound the garlic and chili peppers together into a coarse paste. This releases the oils for maximum flavor.

2

Saut√© and Sear

Heat a wok over high heat with a splash of oil. Add the garlic-chili paste and fry for 20 seconds until fragrant. Add the chicken breast and stir-fry until cooked through.

3

Season and Serve

Add soy sauce, fish sauce, and a pinch of sugar. Toss in the basil leaves and turn off the heat immediately. The residual heat will wilt the basil perfectly.
